Sushi

Sushi is essenially seasoned raw fish slices served with steamed sticky rice and sauce. This dish originated in Japan and has been popular among tourists ever since. Famous fish marktes like the Tsukiji market of Japan profit from the hundreds of Sushi restaurants buying fresh fish from them on a daily basis. The gaining popularity of Sushi encouraged people outside Japan to set up their own restaurants, like we did. We serve a wide range fishes from salmon to tuna at affordable prices. Back to top.
